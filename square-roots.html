Method 04 (Universal)
Estimation Formula

"Square roots of non-perfect numbers don't have to be a mystery. Find the nearest square, and use this simple correction: Root ≈ Perfect Root + (Difference / 2 × Perfect Root)."

Problem: √40 (Approx)
1. Nearest Square
√36 = 6
36 is the closest perfect square below 40. Our base is 6.
2. The Gap
40 - 36 = 4
40 is 4 units away from our base square.
3. The Fraction
4 / (2 × 6)
4 / 12 = 0.33
Divide the gap (4) by double the base (12).
6.33
