Estimation Formula

"For non-perfect cubes, we use a similar correction formula as square roots, but adjusted for the 3rd dimension: Root ≈ Perfect Root + (Gap / 3 × Root²)."

Problem: ∛30 (Approx)
1. Nearest Cube
∛27 = 3
27 is the closest cube. Our base is 3.
2. The Gap
30 - 27 = 3
The number is 3 units away from our perfect base.
3. Correction
3 / (3 × 3²)
3 / 27 ≈ 0.11
Divide gap (3) by triple the square of the root (27).
